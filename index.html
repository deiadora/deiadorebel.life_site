what these are

These stories started because the world kept insisting on separation.

In September 2013, something cracked open. The journeys that followed — first as private field notes, then as novellas — were attempts to map what it actually felt like to be a coherent system moving through a world that ran on different code.

Each book is a first-person journey through a different principle captured in the title. Each chapter has a poem. Each poem is also a song — available at deiadorebel.com.

They were written between 2016 and 2019 and sold in person at farmer's markets and music festivals, where the author would tell people: be a full-length mirror, not a compact. The readers always knew exactly what that meant. They still do.

The research that runs through all of it lives at The Quantum CEO. The practical keys are at I Am Deiadora. This is where the fiction lives — and where it always was.
