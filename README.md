### what this is
 
The publishing home of The Book of Shadows series — five first-person novellas written between 2016 and 2019, sold in person at farmer's markets and music festivals, and now finding their way back into the world with fresh eyes and considerably more awareness.
 
Each book is a first-person journey through a different principle captured in the title. Each chapter has a poem. Each poem is also a song — available at [deiadorebel.com](https://deiadorebel.com).
 
The research that runs through all of it lives at [The Quantum CEO](https://thequantumceo.com). The practical keys are at [I Am Deiadora](https://iamdeiadora.com). This is where the fiction lives — and where it always was.

### the origin
 
In September 2013, something cracked open. The journeys that followed — first as private field notes, then as novellas — were attempts to map what it actually felt like to be a coherent system moving through a world that ran on different code.
 
They were sold in person at farmer's markets and music festivals where the author would tell people: *be a full-length mirror, not a compact.* The readers always knew exactly what that meant. They still do.

### the node
 
**Node 1 · coherence · torus** — quantum coherence. A system in coherence maintains a stable phase relationship between its components. Compression, not decoherence, is what happens when a coherent system interacts with a disordered environment. Decoherent systems fragment. Coherent systems under pressure compress — storing coherence rather than losing it — and restore when conditions allow.
 
The torus is the geometry of a self-sustaining field — it folds back through its own centre, takes its own output and feeds it back as input. It is the geometry of a system that has learned, or remembered, that it contains everything it needs.
 
These books were written from inside that compression. They are what the torus does when it is not yet ready to expand — it tells stories.
